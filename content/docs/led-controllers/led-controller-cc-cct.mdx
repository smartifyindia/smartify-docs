## Technical Overview

A constant current LED controller for CCT (tunable white) lighting fixtures, providing independent control of brightness and colour temperature across warm white and cool white channels.

Designed specifically for constant current LED loads. Not compatible with constant voltage LED strips. Suitable for panel or fixture-integrated installation.

Key Specifications
- Output: CC (Constant Current)
- LED Support: CCT (WW + CW)
- Channels: 2 (WW + CW)
- Control: Brightness + colour temperature
- Installation: Indoor panel or fixture-integrated

⚠ For constant current LED loads only. Not compatible with constant voltage LED strips.
