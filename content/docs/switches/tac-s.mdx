## Overview

A socket that fits the system.

The TAC S is a power socket built for the TAC ecosystem — matching your switches in finish and material, and smart when it needs to be.

## Technical Overview

A modular 6A power socket designed for integration within the TAC Tile system. Not a standalone smart socket — smart control requires connection to a TAC load button channel or a compatible retrofit relay module.

Key Specifications
- Rating: 6A
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold
- Installation: Wall-mounted modular

⚠ Not smart by itself. Smart control requires a TAC load channel or compatible retrofit relay module.
