## Technical Overview

A single-button Zigbee smart switch for direct load control or scene triggering. Features a backlit button with customizable icon and is available in polypropylene (PP) or anti-glare glass (AG) in black, white, or gold.

Dimming can be achieved via compatible retrofit dimmer modules. Operates as a direct load switch or scene trigger within the Zigbee ecosystem.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 1
- Relay: 10A
- Direct Load: Up to 4A
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
