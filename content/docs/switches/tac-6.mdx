## Overview

Six scenes. One wall.

The TAC 6 is a scene controller — not a switch. Press once and an entire room responds. Morning scene, evening scene, away, welcome. Six possibilities on a single, elegant surface.

## Technical Overview

A 6-button Zigbee scene control switch for triggering scenes and automations within a Zigbee ecosystem. Contains no relays and does not switch electrical loads directly.

Each button is independently configurable for scene or automation triggers. Backlit with customizable icons. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 6
- Function: Scene / automation triggers only
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ No relay — does not switch electrical loads. Scene control only.
