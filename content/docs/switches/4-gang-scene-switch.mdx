## Overview

Four buttons. Twelve possibilities. Zero wiring.

Place it anywhere. Stick it to a wall, put it on a nightstand, keep it in a pocket. The 4 Gang Scene Switch gives you up to 12 scenes from four buttons — no installation, no electrician, no limits on where it goes.

## Technical Overview

A battery-powered Zigbee scene switch with 4 mechanical buttons, each supporting single press, double press, and long press actions — enabling up to 12 configurable scenes or automation triggers without wiring.

CR2430 battery with approximately 2-year life. Slim profile (~1.3 cm). Wireless installation via adhesive mount or portable use.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 4 mechanical
- Actions: Single / double / long press per button (12 scenes total)
- Power: CR2430 (~2 years)
- Profile: ~1.3 cm slim
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wireless, adhesive or portable

⚠ No relay — does not switch loads. Scene and automation triggers only.
