## Overview

Your geyser. On your schedule.

The TAC HL puts your heavy appliances — water heater, pump, high-power loads — on a smart switch rated for the job. Set schedules, automate by presence, or control remotely. Same simplicity, for serious loads.

## Technical Overview

A single-button Zigbee smart switch rated for heavy electrical loads up to 20A — designed for direct control of geysers, water heaters, pumps, and other high-power appliances.

Backlit with customizable icon. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.
