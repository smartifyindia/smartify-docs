## Overview

Turn to dim. Push to switch. That's it.

The TAC K makes lighting control physical again — but smarter. A single rotary dial that adjusts brightness, shifts colour temperature, and switches between rooms or groups. No screen. No menu. Just touch.

## Technical Overview

A Zigbee rotary smart dial for intuitive control of lighting brightness, colour temperature, and device groups. Supports up to 3 individual lights or 3 lighting groups via rotate and push interaction.

Rotate to adjust brightness or colour temperature. Push to switch between groups or trigger actions. Designed for wall-mounted installation.

Key Specifications
- Interface: Rotary + push
- Control: Brightness, colour temperature, group switching
- Scope: Up to 3 lights or groups
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ Does not switch electrical loads directly. Smart control interface only.
