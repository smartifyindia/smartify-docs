## Overview

One home. Every ecosystem.

Zigbee devices. Matter devices. Apple HomeKit. The Matter Gateway connects them all — no friction, no switching between apps. Your home speaks one language, even when your devices don't.

## Technical Overview

A wired smart home gateway that bridges Zigbee 3.0 and Matter protocols, enabling unified control across both ecosystems through a single stable Ethernet connection.

Supports up to 128 devices and provides device pairing, cross-protocol bridging, scene automation, and remote control. Enables integration with Matter-enabled applications and Apple HomeKit via Matter.

Key Specifications
- Protocols: Zigbee 3.0 + Matter (IP / Thread bridge)
- Network: Wired Ethernet (LAN)
- Capacity: Up to 128 devices
- Compatibility: Matter apps, Apple HomeKit (via Matter)
- Installation: Indoor, fixed LAN connection

⚠ Does not control electrical loads. Acts as a communication and bridging layer only.
