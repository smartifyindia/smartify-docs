## Technical Overview

A constant voltage LED dimmer controller for single-color low-voltage LED strips, providing smooth 0-100% brightness control through a compatible smart ecosystem.

Requires an appropriate constant voltage DC power supply (12V or 24V) matched to the LED strip specification. Designed for panel or concealed installation.

Key Specifications
- Output: CV (Constant Voltage)
- LED Support: Single-color
- Channels: 1
- Control: 0-100% dimming
- Installation: Indoor panel or concealed

⚠ Requires an appropriate CV DC power supply (12V/24V). Does not power LED strips directly from mains voltage.
