## Overview

Your blinds open with the morning. They close before the afternoon heat sets in. You didn't touch them.

The Shutter Controller brings your motorized curtains, roller blinds, and shutters into your smart home — responding to schedules, conditions, or a single tap. One device, quietly managing the light and privacy of your space. Fits behind your current wall switch. Disappears into your home.

## Technical Overview

A single-channel Zigbee shutter controller designed for AC motorized curtains, roller blinds, and shutters. Provides interlocked directional outputs — Up, Down, and Stop — eliminating any possibility of simultaneous conflicting signals to the motor.

Installs behind the existing wall switch in a flush box or on DIN rail. Communicates natively over Zigbee 3.0 with full Home Assistant support, and integrates with Matter and Apple HomeKit via a compatible hub.

Key Specifications
- Channels: 1 motor channel
- Control Type: Interlocked directional (Up / Down / Stop)
- Max Load: 2A
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Compatible with: Home Assistant (native), Matter & Apple HomeKit (via hub)
- Installation: Flush box or DIN rail

⚠ Designed exclusively for AC motor loads. Not suitable for DC motors or use as a lighting relay.
