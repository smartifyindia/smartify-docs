## Technical Overview

A heavy-duty single-channel Zigbee relay rated for direct switching of high-current loads up to 40A. Designed for high-power appliances such as water heaters, pumps, HVAC loads, and industrial equipment.

Installs behind an existing wall switch in a flush box or on DIN rail. Proper cable sizing, circuit protection, and installation practices are mandatory for safe operation at rated current. Communicates natively over Zigbee 3.0 with Home Assistant, and integrates with Matter and Apple HomeKit via a compatible hub.
