## Overview

Open. Close. Stop. That's all you need.

The TAC SH gives your motorized blinds and curtains a dedicated, physical control — three buttons, completely intuitive, available in the finish that suits your space.

## Technical Overview

A 3-button Zigbee shutter and curtain control switch with dedicated Open, Close, and Stop functions for AC motorized blinds and curtains up to 150W.

Backlit with customizable icons. Available in PP or anti-glare glass in black, white, or gold.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 3 (Open / Close / Stop)
- Motor: AC curtain/blind, up to 150W
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Materials: PP or AG glass
- Colours: Black, White, Gold
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ AC motor loads only. Not suitable for use as a standard lighting switch.
