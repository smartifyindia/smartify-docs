## Technical Overview

A Wi-Fi IR blaster that sends infrared commands to legacy IR-controlled devices — TVs, air conditioners, set-top boxes, and other appliances — through your smart home network.

Connects to your local 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network and transmits configurable IR codes via mobile apps and automation platforms. Requires line-of-sight to target devices.

Key Specifications
- Protocol: Wi-Fi 2.4 GHz
- Control: Infrared (IR)
- Installation: Indoor, line-of-sight placement

⚠ IR line-of-sight to target devices is required. Does not switch electrical loads. RF devices not supported — see IR+RF Blaster.
