## Overview

Control your room. Charge your devices. One panel.

The TOQ 4T+1S combines smart control with everyday utility — four touch buttons for your home automation, a power socket, and USB charging, all in a single elegant wall panel.

## Technical Overview

A 4-button Zigbee capacitive touch panel with an integrated 6A power socket and USB-C/USB-A charging ports, combining smart load control with utility power access in a single wall panel.

Each button is independently configurable for toggle or momentary operation, supporting load switching, scene triggering, and dimmer control. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel options.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 4 capacitive touch
- Socket: 1 integrated (6A)
- USB: 1x USB-C + 1x USB-A
- Relay: 10A per channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A use an external SSR or contactor. Socket rated at 6A maximum.
