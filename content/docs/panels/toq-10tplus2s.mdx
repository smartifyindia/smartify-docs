## Overview

The TOQ 10T+2S is the most complete single-panel solution in the range — ten touch points, two power outlets, and a finish that belongs on any wall.

## Technical Overview

A 10-button Zigbee capacitive touch panel with two integrated 6A utility sockets, providing comprehensive smart control across 10 channels alongside dual power outlet access.

Each button independently controls a load or triggers a scene. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 10 capacitive touch
- Sockets: 2 integrated (6A each)
- Relay: 10A per button channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per button channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A, use an external SSR or contactor. Each socket rated at 6A.
