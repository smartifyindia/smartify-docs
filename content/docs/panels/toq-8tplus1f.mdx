## Overview

Lights and fans. One elegant panel.

Eight touch points for your lighting and scenes, plus a dedicated fan control — all in one glass panel. The TOQ 8T+1F handles the full room without compromise.

## Technical Overview

An 8-button Zigbee touch panel with a dedicated fan control channel for ceiling fans up to 120W, combining multi-circuit load control with integrated fan management in a single wall panel.

Each of the 8 buttons is independently configurable for toggle or momentary operation. The fan channel operates separately for speed and on/off control. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 8 capacitive touch
- Fan Channels: 1 (up to 120W)
- Relay: 10A per button channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per button channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A on button channels, use an external SSR or contactor.
