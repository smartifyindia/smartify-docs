## Overview

Everything a room needs. One panel.

Lighting control, two ceiling fans, and a power socket — all from a single wall panel. The TOQ 4T+2F+1S is built for the rooms where you need it all.

## Technical Overview

A multi-function Zigbee wall panel combining 4 load control buttons, 2 dedicated fan channels, and an integrated 6A utility socket in a single glass panel.

Ideal for rooms requiring lighting, fan, and power access control from one location. Each button configurable independently. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 4 capacitive touch
- Fan Channels: 2 (up to 120W each)
- Socket: 1 integrated (6A)
- Relay: 10A per button channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A, use an external SSR or contactor. Socket rated at 6A.
