## Overview

Eight circuits. One surface.

A full room — or an entire floor — controlled from a single panel. The TOQ 8T gives you eight independent touch points, finished in glass, responding to the lightest touch.

## Technical Overview

An 8-button Zigbee capacitive touch panel for load switching, scene triggering, and dimming control across up to 8 independent channels.

Each button is individually configurable for toggle or momentary operation. Supports dimming via compatible retrofit dimmer modules. Available in white or black glass with silver, gold, or black bezel and configurable backlight colour.

Key Specifications
- Buttons: 8 capacitive touch
- Relay: 10A per channel
- Direct Load: Up to 4A per channel
- Protocol: Zigbee 3.0
- Finish: White/Black glass + Silver/Gold/Black bezel
- Installation: Wall-mounted

⚠ For loads above 4A, use an external SSR or contactor.
